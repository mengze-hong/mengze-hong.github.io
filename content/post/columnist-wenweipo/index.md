I'm thrilled to announce my new role as a columnist for Wen Wei Po, where I'll dive into the transformative intersection of AI and Web3 in the era of large language models (LLMs). My series, starting July 15, 2025, explores how LLMs are revolutionizing user experiences and enterprise efficiency in the Web3 ecosystem.


### Part 1: [AI-Powered User Experiences](https://www.wenweipo.com/a/202507/15/AP68756546e4b0eba3f09a8f8a.html)

LLMs have democratized AI, enabling intuitive tools for everyday users. From intelligent chatbots in crypto exchanges to personalized NFT recommendations, AI enhances Web3 platforms by streamlining interactions, boosting engagement, and strengthening security through real-time threat detection.


### Part 2: [Enterprise Efficiency in Web3](https://www.wenweipo.com/a/202507/22/AP687e9e96e4b0fced478dc816.html)

For Web3 businesses, LLMs optimize operations, cutting costs and enhancing decision-making. From real-time market monitoring to liquidity management in DeFi protocols, AI drives efficiency. Automated content creation and community management further empower platforms and influencers.


### Part 3: [Universal vs. Specialized Models](https://www.wenweipo.com/a/202507/29/AP6887daa1e4b06398ab355d9b.html)

Universal LLMs excel in broad tasks but struggle with Web3-specific challenges like blockchain data analysis. Specialized models, tailored for Web3, offer precision in smart contract audits and compliance but require high development costs. Combining both unlocks the full potential of AI in Web3.
Stay tuned for more insights on how AI is shaping the decentralized future!
